What is Agentic Ai:
  . It is an AI system that can make decisions and take actions on its own to achieve a goal without being told exactly what to do at every steps.
  . It is not only provide answer to your question but can do actions.

Steps:
  . Goal oriented planning
  . Multi-step reasoning
  . Autonomous decision making
  . Tool, Knowledge, Memory.

Python Agentic Ai Module:
  . pip install agno (https://pypi.org/project/agno/)

Agentic AI by drag and drop:
  . Zapier (https://zapier.com/)
  . n8n (https://n8n.io/)
